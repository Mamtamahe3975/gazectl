## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### The app does not open

Try these steps:

1. Check that you downloaded the latest release.
2. Open the file again from your Downloads folder.

4. Make sure macOS did not block it.
5. Restart your Mac and try again.

### The camera does not work

Try these steps:

1. Open System Settings.
2. Check camera permission for gazectl.
3. Close other apps that use the camera.
4. Unplug and reconnect your camera if you use an external one.
5. Test the camera in another app first.

### Focus does not switch

Try these steps:

1. Check that head tracking is active.
2. Make sure the app has the right permissions.
3. Confirm your display setup is detected by macOS.
4. Move your head more clearly from side to side.
5. Lower the sensitivity if the app jumps too fast.

### The app reacts in the wrong direction

Try changing the direction mapping in the app settings. If left and right feel reversed, swap them and test again.
